Soft Pretzel

Description

This is an easy recipe to make those delicious soft baked german pretzels that are perfect for an afternoon snack! Make it easy at home and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups water, warm
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 4 ½ cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons oil, divided
  • ⅔ cup baking soda
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • coarse salt
